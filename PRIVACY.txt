VoxWild — Privacy Policy
============================
Cookie Studios | Last updated: April 2026

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
THE SHORT VERSION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

VoxWild collects no personal data. All audio generation runs entirely on
your computer. Nothing you type or record ever leaves your machine.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
WHAT WE COLLECT
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

We collect nothing. Specifically:

  - No text you enter into VoxWild is transmitted anywhere.
  - No audio you generate or record is transmitted anywhere.
  - No voice clone samples are uploaded to any server.
  - No usage statistics, telemetry, or crash reports are sent to us.
  - No analytics or tracking of any kind is present in the software.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
LICENSE ACTIVATION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

When you activate your license key, VoxWild makes a single HTTPS request
to the Gumroad API to validate your key. This request includes:

  - Your license key (so it can be verified)
This is the only network request the software ever makes. No other data is
sent. Gumroad's privacy policy governs how they handle this data:
https://gumroad.com/privacy

If you have not activated a license key, no network requests are made at all.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
LOCAL DATA STORAGE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

VoxWild stores the following files on your computer only (legacy folder
name "TTS Studio" preserved from earlier versions so existing user data
and model installs are not disrupted):

  %APPDATA%\TTS Studio\settings.json   — your app preferences
  %APPDATA%\TTS Studio\tts_profiles.json — saved EQ/voice presets
  %APPDATA%\TTS Studio\calibration.json — audio calibration data
  %APPDATA%\TTS Studio\license.json    — your activated license key
  %APPDATA%\TTS Studio\voice_clones\   — voice clone audio samples you add
  %APPDATA%\TTS Studio\crashes.log     — local crash log (never transmitted)

These files never leave your machine unless you choose to copy them yourself.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
AI MODELS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

All AI models (Kokoro TTS, Chatterbox TTS) run locally on your hardware.
No audio or text is sent to any AI cloud service. The models are downloaded
once at setup and then used entirely offline.
